Set CogiSearch as Your Default Search Engine

Firefox
Chrome
Edge

For Firefox:

  1. Open Firefox preferences by typing about:preferences#search in your address bar
  2. Scroll down to the "Default Search Engine" section
  3. Find "CogiSearch" in the dropdown and select it

For Chrome:

  1. Open Chrome settings by typing chrome://settings/searchEngines in your address bar
  2. Scroll down to "Search engines" and click "Manage search engines and site search"
  3. Find "CogiSearch" under "Other search engines" (if you've used it before)
  4. Click the three dots next to CogiSearch and select "Make default"
  5. If CogiSearch is not listed, click "Add" and enter:
    • Search engine: CogiSearch
    • Shortcut: cs
    • URL: https://search.cogilabs.eu/?q=%s

For Edge:

  1. Open Edge settings by typing edge://settings/searchEngines in your address bar
  2. Click "Manage search engines"
  3. Find "CogiSearch" in the list (if you've used it before)
  4. Click the three dots next to CogiSearch and select "Make default"
  5. If CogiSearch is not listed, click "Add" and enter:
    • Search engine: CogiSearch
    • Shortcut: cs
    • URL: https://search.cogilabs.eu/?q=%s

About Custom Bangs Sync

This extension automatically synchronizes your custom bangs across browsers when you're signed in. All your custom bangs created on the CogiSearch website will be stored in your browser's synced storage.
