Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CogiSearch ("we", "our", or "the service") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our search service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your data is handled.

Information We Collect

CogiSearch is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect and process minimal information:

Search Queries

When you use CogiSearch:

  • We do not store your search queries. When you enter a search query, it is only processed to redirect you to the appropriate search engine.
  • We do not maintain search logs that could be used to track your search history or preferences.
  • We do not create user profiles based on your search behavior.

Client-Side Storage

CogiSearch uses browser storage mechanisms to enhance your experience:

  • Local Storage: We use browser localStorage to save your preferences, including custom bangs, theme settings, and your preferred default search engine. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.
  • DuckDuckGo Bangs Cache: We cache the DuckDuckGo bangs list in your browser's localStorage to improve performance, which is refreshed approximately once every 24 hours.

How We Use Your Data

The limited data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Service Functionality: To provide the core features of CogiSearch, including redirection to your preferred search engine and custom bang support.
  • User Preferences: To remember your settings (theme, default search engine, etc.) entirely within your browser.

Data Sharing

CogiSearch does not share your data with third parties because we don't collect or store data on our servers. When you use CogiSearch:

  • Your search query is redirected to your chosen search engine, and then you interact directly with that search engine.
  • The search engine you are redirected to (such as DuckDuckGo, Google, Bing, etc.) will receive your search query directly. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for information about how they handle your data.

Browser Extension

The CogiSearch browser extension offers additional functionality:

  • The extension synchronizes your custom bangs across your devices using your browser's built-in sync functionality.
  • For details about the extension's privacy practices, please refer to the Extension Privacy Policy.

Analytics and Cookies

CogiSearch does not use any third-party analytics services or cookies to track users. We do not analyze your search behavior or collect usage statistics.

Your Data Rights

You have control over the data stored in your browser:

  • Access and Control: All data is accessible through your browser's developer tools or settings.
  • Deletion: You can clear all CogiSearch data by clearing your browser's localStorage for the CogiSearch domain.
  • No Account Needed: We do not require accounts, so there is no personal data to request or export.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The latest version will always be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
